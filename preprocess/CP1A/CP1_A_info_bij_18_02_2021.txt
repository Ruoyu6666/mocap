"Hip" markets are directly over hip bone
"Back" markers are directly over shoulderblades
"Ankle" represents lower tibia bone near ankle joint
"Knee" represents higher tibia bone near knee joint
there may be recordings where markers is missing (mouse lost piercing during experiment)

Length of markers in this experiment may not be consistent, 6-8 mm with 3-4 mm ball 
(in later experiemnts markers are 6 mm (shoulder or coordinate),7 mm (hips), 8 mm (legs) long with 3 mm ball but 5 mm bals are used for experiments)
